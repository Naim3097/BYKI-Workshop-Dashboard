Good to know

Common questions

Is the 3D simulator my actual gearbox?
It's an accurate, physics-based model of a Punch VT2/VT3 CVT — the type used in many Proton and Perodua cars. It's there so you can understand how a CVT works and what we inspect. Your real diagnosis happens in the workshop on your car.
How much does a CVT diagnostic cost?
Message us on WhatsApp with your car model and symptoms for current pricing. The diagnostic fee is typically credited toward the repair if you go ahead.
Do you handle my car brand?
We service most CVT and automatic transmissions — Proton, Perodua, Honda, Nissan, Toyota, Mitsubishi and more. Send us your model and we'll confirm.
My car is in limp mode — can you help?
Yes. Limp mode usually means a stored fault has put the transmission into a safe state. We'll read the codes, explain them, and tell you what it'll take to clear it properly.
Is there a warranty?
Every repair and rebuild comes with a 12-month workmanship warranty. Parts carry their own manufacturer warranty on top.
